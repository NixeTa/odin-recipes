recipes for meatball

in this pages i want to share the recipes to make a meatball

here is the ingredients :

  • 500 grams of ground beef
  • 130 grams of tapioca flour
  • 7 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fried shallots
  • 2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoon of mushroom broth powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of ground pepper
  • ice cubes as needed

here is the steps to make meatball :

  1. Prepare the ingredients
    • Ensure all ingredients are ready and measured according to the recipe.
  2. Make the meatbal Mixture
    • In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, tapioca flour, minced garlic, fried shallots, salt, mushroom broth powder, egg white, and ground pepper.
    • Mix all the ingredients thoroughly. You can use your hands or a mixer on low speed.
    • Gradually add ice cubes while mixing until the mixture becomes elastic and easy to shape.
  3. Shape the Meatballs
    • Take a small amount of the mixture and form it into small balls using your hands. You can use a spoon to help shape the balls.
    • Place the meatballs on a tray lightly dusted with tapioca flour to prevent sticking.
  4. Boil the Meatballs
    • Bring a large pot of water to boil.
    • Drop the meatballs into the boiling water. Cook until the meatballs float to the surface, indicating they are cooked through.
    • Remove the meatballs and drain.
  5. Serve
    • The meatballs are ready to be served with broth, noodles, and other accompaniments like green onions, celery, and fried shallots.
