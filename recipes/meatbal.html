recipes for meatball

in this pages i want to share the recipes to make a meatball

here is the ingredients :

  • 500 grams of ground beef
  • 130 grams of tapioca flour
  • 7 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of fried shallots
  • 2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoon of mushroom broth powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of ground pepper
  • ice cubes as needed
